About SnapJot Beta

SnapJot is a new iOS app designed for iPhones and iPads

Have you ever found yourself taking a picture and wishing you could associate some text with it? For example, you are shopping and take a picture of an item that you would like to show someone later. But you also wish to take some notes: perhaps price or product-code. Where do you put that text? You can use another app to enter these details, but how do you pair it with the image later ?


Or perhaps you are in a social situation and take a group picture. You want to capture some information about where you are, what you are doing and who you are with, but you don't want to wait until later to reconstruct it. Wouldn't it be useful to capture it at the moment you took the picture?


SnapJot solves these problems by allowing you to hand-write some text and have it appear directly in your image:



You use your finger to write your message. No keyboard required! It's quicker and more expressive. For social media, the effect is immediate and striking, and the text you write cannot be readily mined for advertising purposes.
