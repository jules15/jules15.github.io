Collagio Privacy Policy


1. What information does Collagio collect?

Collagio does not request, or collect, any personally identifiable information from you.

2. What about third parties?

Collagio allows you to share the pictures you create via the “Share” feature of Android. No information, besides the picture itself, is transmitted to the receiving application. Each receiving application is governed by its own privacy policy.

Collagio may gather non-identifiable from your phone, including brand, model, Android version, etc., which may include identifiers that are unique to your phone.

3. How is any data you collect used?

Any data collected about your device is used solely for:

a. Quality Assurance, to improve the reliability and stability of future version of Collagio.

b. Analytics, allowing us to understand how our users are distributed around the world, what kind of phones they use, etc.

4. How do we protect your information?

Any information we collect is stored and secured using prevailing security standards and techniques.

5. Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, ot otherwise transfer any anonymized information to outside parties, other than those required for us to provide our services, as alluded to in section 2. We reserve the right to use anonymized information we collect to comply with law enforcement.

6. Do we use cookies or other tracking information?

No, we do not employ any tracking technologies.

7. Scope of this policy

This policy applies to the Collagio application only.
