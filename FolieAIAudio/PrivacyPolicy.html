Folie AI Audio Privacy Policy


1. What information does Folie AI Audio collect?

Folie AI Audio does not request, or collect, any personally identifiable information from you.

2. What about third parties?

Folie AI Audio allows you to share the audio clips you create via the iOS “share sheet”. No information, besides the audio itself, is transmitted to any receiving application. Receiving applications are governed by their own privacy policy.

Folie AI Audio may gather non-identifiable information from your device, including model, iOS version, etc. However, this may include identifiers that are unique to your device.

3. How is any data you collect used?

Any data collected about your device is used solely for:

a. Crash reporting: Anonymized crash data may be sent to Apple for quality-assurance purposes.

b. Analytics: Anonymized information about how customers use our products.

4. Information Security

All anonymized data is stored and secured using prevailing security standards.

5. Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any anonymized information to outside parties, other than those required for us to provide our services, as described in section 2. We reserve the right to use anonymized information we collect to comply with law enforcement.

6. Do we use cookies or other tracking information?

No, we do not employ any tracking technologies.

7. Scope of this policy

This policy applies to the Folie AI Audio application only.

By using Folie AI Audio, you are consenting to this privacy policy.
